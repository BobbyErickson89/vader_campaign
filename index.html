About Darth

Darth Vader has proven himself to be a leader in the Galactic Empire since it's conception after the successful take down of the Jedi Council. Using his strength, wit, and sheer power of the Force, he has overseen successful campaigns such as the construction of the Death Star and the execution of Order 66.

Along with being a cunning overlord for the Empire, Darth is a caring family man. He constantly boasts of his twin children, Luke and Leia. He hopes to have Luke by his side during his campaign, so that they can rule the galaxy together as father and son.

Emperor Palpatine has shown a strong interest in Darth since he was a young Padowan, and has since become a valuable apprentice to the emperor. However, it is time for a new galactic overlord. Join Darth on the dark side of the Force and together, we can overthrow the emperor, destroy some Rebel scum, and claim Darth as our new Galactic Overlord!
